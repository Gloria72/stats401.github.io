Unveiling Health Patterns in 2017 through NHANES Data

Made by Yu Cheng, Xueting Li, Hengchen Liu

Dietary, Drugs, Blood Pressure.

In the pursuit of understanding the intricate patterns of dietary habits, blood pressure, and drug use, the National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey (NHANES) stands as a beacon of insight. The NHANES dataset from 2017-2018 offers a panoramic view of the health landscape of the population, capturing crucial information related to dietary habits, blood pressure levels, and the prevalence of drug use before the epidemic. As we delve into this wealth of data, our primary aim is to uncover meaningful patterns and correlations that can inform public health strategies and individual wellness decisions. The three focal points of our investigation—dietary data, blood pressure metrics, and drug utilization—serve as integral components in comprehending the multifaceted nature of health in our society.
